United States Defense Spending

The US has historically allocated a very large proportion of its total GDP towards military expenditure when compared to the rest of the world. For example, in 2018 the United States alone accounted for approximately 36.4% of total military expenditures.

In the below pie chart, the total defense spending of the United States is compared to the rest of the world combined in 2018.
